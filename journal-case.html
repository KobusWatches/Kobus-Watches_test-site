2 Feb 2026 · Design

The case: oval, rectangular, and under 36mm

Exact dimensions are still being finalised. The direction: a small, thin case, under 36mm and under 10mm thick.

Most watches start from a circle. Kobus starts from a shaped case — oval and rectangular directions are both on the table — because the organic curve is where the Art Nouveau reference lives. The exact dimensions will depend on the final form.

What is already fixed is the envelope: under 36mm across and under 10mm in total thickness. Small enough to disappear on the wrist, thin enough to slide under a cuff. Proportion over presence.

The lugs and crown are being drawn as continuations of the case line rather than attachments to it — the same way Horta's ironwork grows out of the structure it supports.
